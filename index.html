    Project Overview
    Our previous projects were mostly service oriented, so as a team we thought it would be interesting to come up with a design solution that was centered around a physical product rather than a service. With it's rising popularity, the smart speaker was a perfect product for us to identify business challenges that could be turned into opportunities.
    We looked at the top competitors in the smart speaker market (Amazon Echo, Google Home, and Apple HomePod), researching respective sale statistics and the average smart speaker owner. Although the Amazon Echo is the leading seller of smart speakers, Google Home and other competitors are growing at faster rates and projected to overtake the Amazon Echo in 2018. It is clear that Amazon needs to adapt in order to remain a viable competitor.
    Defining the Problem
    Statistics from comScore and canalys show that while the Amazon Echo was the most popular smart speaker in the U.S., Google Home has surpassed Amazon Echo globally, both in market dominance and growth. In the graph below, you can see how the smart speaker market is expected to change. Notice, that both Amazon's competitors and the total market size are growing at an exponential rate.
